Making Classic Fried Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 1 pound uncooked jumbo shrimp
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 tablespoon black pepper
  • 3 large eggs
  • vegetable oil (for deep frying

Method

  1. pour the vegetable oil into sauce pan and preheat
  2. beat the eggs into a mixing bowl
  3. mix the dry ingredients
    • flour
    • pepper and salt
  4. take the shrimp and wash it properly
  5. dip the shrimp in he whipped egg
  6. dab with flour generously all over
  7. when properly coated , put a tiny amount of shrimp to test if oil is hot enough
  8. when oil is hot enough deep fry ffor abouut 3 minutes
  9. serve and garnish with parsley
